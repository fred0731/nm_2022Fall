    Let me introduce you the CALENTOUR, a board game concept calendar based on the idea of "Let's spend my day like a trip and record it!" Like traveling all over the world, why don't you collect a year's records and travel diary through my memories? Fill the 2023 calendar with the theme of your own memories and daily life.


    CALENTOUR was created to make our daily lives preciously remembered as travelers of life. Unlike a regular calendar, it is not a calendar that goes over a day by looking at the date, but a calendar that compiles and records a month's memories with your hands!
